The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Deadpool & Wolverine

This movie will feature the return of Hugh Jackman's Wolverine, teaming up with Ryan Reynolds' Deadpool in a highly anticipated, action-packed crossover. Fans are excited for the dynamic and humorous clash between the two iconic Marvel characters.

Weathering with You

Weathering With You is a 2019 anime film directed by Makoto Shinkai, centering on a runaway boy who meets a girl with the power to control the weather. The story beautifully blends romance and fantasy, exploring themes of love, sacrifice, and the impact of climate change.

Tumbadd

“Tumbbad” is a 2018 Indian horror-fantasy film that follows a man's quest for hidden treasure in a cursed village, haunted by a vengeful ancient deity. The movie blends folklore and eerie visuals, exploring themes of greed and the consequences of unchecked desires.
